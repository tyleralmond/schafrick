Sculpting Liquids

It’s not unusual to find Peter Schafrick
ankle deep in paint or oil or spirits.
With a penchant for planned spontane-
ity, Peter is a product specialist who
shoots liquids splashed and poured in
air. The resulting photos of dynamic
sculptural forms are imbued with an
aesthetic lustre that elevates commercial
representation to high art.

Pouring Images

Affiliated with Toronto’s Sugino Studios,
Peter has added digital cinema to his
artistic repertoire. His dazzling splashes
and pours are filmed in ultra slow motion
using very sophisticated high-speed
camera technology. Capturing the
beauty of liquids moving through space,
Peter's film work completes a product
offering now suitable for multiple
platforms—from print to television, from
moving billboards to the web.

Intensifying Objects

Peter’s interest in capturing fluids in
motion arose out of still life work intent
on revealing the hidden vitality of objects
at rest. With a portfolio as strongly
represented by still lifes as by liquids,
he continues to shoot product stills
reverberating with a clean, elegant,
modern sensibility.

Winning Recognition

Peter displays the gifted autodidact’s
passion for his craft and willingness to
experiment. The excitement he brings
to working ideas into striking new images
stands behind his growing list of high-
profile clients and an international
reputation for innovation. His work has
received several major international
awards and been featured in leading
publications on photography.

Delivering Wholeness

Peter Schafrick Photography offers
a complete, integrated service.
It encompasses all aspects of production
and post-production work in both still
photography and digital cinema. Peter is
supported by an in-house team of expert
technicians, with whom he has worked
for many years. The team provides
extensive technical assistance. It gener-
ates quick preliminary compositions to
help clients explore possibilities and
visualize outcomes. It also handles the
entire process of post-production
retouching. Our retoucher’s presence at
every phase of the project ensures a
seamless transition from client brief and
photo shoot to finished product.
